Controls:
Drag/touch to steer your tanker up and down the channel
Double-tap to deploy flares (destroys nearby drones & missiles)
Pick up glowing icons:
⚕ Repairs hull · ✦ Adds flares · ⚓ Summons a USN escort that auto-fires
The map is geographically accurate — you pass through the Persian Gulf, squeeze through the real Strait with Qeshm, Hormuz Island, the Tunbs, then exit into the Gulf of Oman. Threat level escalates with each wave.
